Questions

Things people actually ask.

If you don't see your question here, ask in the consultation — there are no dumb ones.

How do I know if I need filler vs Botox?
Botox treats lines from movement (frown, forehead, crows feet). Filler treats volume loss (cheeks, lips, hollow under-eyes). At consultation we'll show you with a mirror exactly which lines fall into which category.
Will I look frozen?
Not unless you ask for that — and even then, we'll talk you down. Our default is conservative dosing in 2–3 areas. You should still be able to raise your eyebrows.
What about side effects?
Bruising and swelling are most common, typically 3–5 days for filler, less for Botox. Rare risks (vascular occlusion, etc.) are reviewed in detail before treatment. We carry hyaluronidase on-site.
How long does it last?
Botox: 3–4 months on average. Filler depends on the product and area: 6–12 months for lips, 12–18 months for cheek and jaw. Bio-stimulators (Sculptra) last up to 2 years.
Do you offer financing?
Yes — Cherry and Care Credit, both with 0% promotional periods. We don't push it. If you can't comfortably afford a treatment now, the right answer is usually to wait.
Can I bring photos of what I want?
Please do. We use them as a conversation starter, not a target — your face has its own architecture and we work with that.
