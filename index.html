Questions

The ones I get most.

Why so much cheaper than US agencies?
No project managers, no account executives, no salespeople. Cost of living in Germany is also lower than San Francisco. The work itself isn't cheaper — the overhead is.
Do you handle hosting and domains?
Yes. Sites are deployed to Vercel or Netlify (both included free under your usage). I help you connect your domain and set up email forwarding. No monthly hosting bill from me.
What about HIPAA / patient data?
For Standard and Premium tiers, all forms route to a HIPAA-compliant provider of your choice (Jotform HIPAA, Boulevard, etc.). The Custom tier includes signed BAAs and HIPAA-conscious infrastructure.
Can you write the copy?
Yes — for the homepage and one service page, included. For full copy across treatment pages, add $1,500. Most owners prefer to write their own with my outline.
What happens if I want changes after launch?
Standard includes 30 days of free tweaks (copy edits, image swaps, small layout changes). After that, I bill $150/hr or you can take an optional $400/mo care plan with 4 hours included.
Do you do SEO and ads?
On-page SEO is part of every build. I don't manage ad campaigns — I'll happily refer you to specialists I trust if you need that.
