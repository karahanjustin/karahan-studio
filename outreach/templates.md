## When they ghost after a discovery call

```
Hey [first name] — circling back on our chat from [day]. No pressure either way, just wanted to check whether the project is still on the table for [month] or if I should reach back out later in the year.
```

---

## Voice for all of this

- Short sentences. Active voice.
- One ask per message.
- No "I hope this finds you well" / "circling back to circle back" / "synergies"
- Their name once, your name once. No more.
- If you wouldn't say it out loud over coffee, don't write it.
